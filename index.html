About Me

My name is Michael but most people call me, Garland. I was raised in Little Rock, Arkansas and I still live with an old friend from my hometown. I love running, improv comedy, and learning to code.

I have performed with numerous improv troupes and on even more stages as a comedian. I've met some of the best friends I've ever had and wouldn't change a thing. Unfortunately, I am taking a break in order to study computer development. But as I said before, "I wouldn't change a thing."
