About Me

My name is Michael but most people call me Garland. I was raised in Little Rock, Arkansas and I still live with an old friend from my hometown. I moved to Atlanta in 2013 and lived in a storage closet for 8 months before getting a bedroom and bed of my own.

After graduating college in 2011 with two degrees, one in Political Science and the other in Writing, I spent two years teaching and writing in Thailand. I traveled all over the country and spent time with over 100 elephants. I also ate my favorite food, kao soi, for lunch every day.

These days I love running, improv comedy, and learning to code.

I have performed with numerous improv troupes and on even more stages as a comedian. I've met some of the best friends I've ever had through comedy and wouldn't change a thing. Unfortunately, I am taking a break in order to study computer development. But as I said before, "I wouldn't change a thing."
